Welcome to Smart Inventory Management

Our Smart Inventory Management System is designed to help you track and manage your inventory effortlessly. Whether you are managing stock for a small shop, a warehouse, or a school store, this system gives you a clear view of your items, categories, and transactions.

With an intuitive interface, you can:

  • Add and manage items: Quickly add new inventory items, assign them to categories, and set quantity and pricing information.
  • Dynamic categories: Choose existing categories from a dropdown or create new ones on the fly, keeping your system organized.
  • Record transactions: Track stock in and stock out events to maintain real-time inventory levels.
  • View reports: Generate simple reports to see item quantities, stock history, and overall inventory health.
  • User-friendly navigation: The menu allows you to jump easily between the dashboard, items, transactions, and reports.

This dashboard acts as your control center, providing guidance on how to use the system efficiently. Explore the Items section to add or delete items, the Transactions section to log stock movements, and the Reports section to visualize your inventory at a glance.

Our goal is to reduce manual work, prevent stock errors, and help you make better inventory decisions with minimal effort.

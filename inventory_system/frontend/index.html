Welcome to Smart Inventory Management

Our Smart Inventory Management System is designed to help you track and manage your inventory effortlessly. Whether you are managing stock for a small shop, a warehouse, or a school store, this system gives you a clear view of your items, categories, and transactions.

With an intuitive interface, you can:

  • Add and manage items: Quickly add new inventory items, assign them to categories, and set quantity and pricing information.
  • Dynamic categories: Choose existing categories or create new ones on the fly.
  • Record transactions: Track stock in and stock out events.
  • View reports: See item quantities and inventory health.
  • User-friendly navigation: Easily move between sections.

This dashboard acts as your control center. Explore the Items, Transactions, and Reports sections to manage your inventory effectively.
